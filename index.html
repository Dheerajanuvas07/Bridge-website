What Bridge does — and does not do

Bridge provides non-medical appointment support. We help with appointment coordination, pickup, walk-in escort, check-in support, waiting, safe return, and family updates.

Bridge does not provide medical advice, medication administration, physical lifting, clinical decisions, or emergency care. If there is an emergency, call 911.
