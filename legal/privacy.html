Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 26, 2026

Plain-English summary: We collect only the information needed to coordinate and deliver non-medical appointment support. This includes logistics, mobility notes, and updates for your trusted contact. We do not sell your personal information.

1) Scope

This Privacy Policy explains how Bridge collects, uses, and shares information when you visit our website or request/receive services.

2) Information we collect

  • Contact info: Name, phone number, and email.
  • Appointment details: Pickup/drop-off addresses, appointment location, and timing.
  • Mobility & safety: Notes on walker/wheelchair use, stairs, or access instructions.
  • Trusted contact: Information for the person authorized to receive the Completion Report.
  • Service notes: The Bridge Completion Report and logs related to service delivery.
  • Communications: Messages and emails related to scheduling.

3) Health information (important)

Bridge is a non-medical service. Please do not send unnecessary medical details. If limited health-related context is shared, such as mobility needs or simple logistics, Bridge uses it only to coordinate safe appointment support. We treat sensitive information with care and minimize data retention for any health-related notes.

4) How we use information

  • Coordinate and deliver services (booking, matching, routing, timing)
  • Safety planning (mobility notes, access instructions)
  • Customer support and issue resolution
  • Send confirmations and updates you request
  • Improve the service and website performance
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, or unsafe behavior

5) How we share information

  • With the Bridge Captain assigned to your service (logistics, mobility notes, and contact info).
  • With the Trusted Contact only when authorized to receive the Completion Report.
  • With service providers that help us operate (e.g., payment processors, booking forms).
  • For legal/safety reasons (to comply with law or respond to an emergency/911 situation).

We do not sell your personal information.

6) Cookies & analytics

We may use cookies or analytics to understand traffic and improve the site. You can typically control cookies in your browser settings.

7) Data retention

We keep information as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal requirements, and resolve disputes. We aim to retain only what we need.

8) Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards. No system is 100% secure, but we work to reduce risk and limit data collection.

9) Your choices

  • Request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information where applicable
  • Opt out of non-essential communications
  • Control cookies through your browser

10) Children's privacy

Bridge services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

11) Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. The "Effective date" above reflects the latest version.

12) Contact
