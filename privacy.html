Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

1. Information We Collect

Bridge collects only the information necessary to facilitate connections between families and helpers. This may include:

  • Names and contact information (phone, email)
  • Service addresses
  • Task details and requirements
  • Feedback and reviews

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Coordinate services and match you with suitable helpers
  • Conduct internal market research to improve our platform
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.
We share data only as needed to coordinate services (e.g., providing your address to a matched helper) or if required by law.

4. Data Security

Information may be stored securely using trusted third-party tools such as Google Forms, secure cloud spreadsheets, and reputable email providers. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data.

5. Your Consent

By using Bridge, you consent to this limited collection and use of your data as described in this policy.

6. Contact

If you have questions about your data, please contact us at hello@bridge-lincoln.com.
